

FOOTBALL FREE KICKS

Supplied data for two more additional in-game statistics:
- Free Kicks
- Free Kicks Attempts

You need to create three plots that portray the following insights:

1- Free Kick Attempts per game
2- Accuracy of Free Kicks
3- Player playing style excluding Free Kicks*

*Each Free Kick is worth 1 point
The data has been supplied in the form of vectors. You will have to create
the two matrices before you proceed with the analysis

The matrix calculation for part 3 is:
(Points - FreeKicks) / FieldGoals
